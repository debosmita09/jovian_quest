Jovian Quest: A Journey Through Jupiter and Its Moons

You are the pilot of a research probe entering the Jupiter system. Your mission: survey Jupiter and its four largest moons — Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto — collecting scientific data along the way.

At each destination, initiate a landing sequence, scan the surface, atmosphere, and science highlights, record your findings in the Mission Log, then complete a knowledge challenge to unlock the next moon. Real photographs captured by NASA spacecraft appear as you touch down, alongside why this giant planet and its moons matter for understanding how our Solar System formed — and where else in it life might exist.
