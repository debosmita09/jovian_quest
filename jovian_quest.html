Jovian Quest: A Journey Through Jupiter and Its Moons

You are the pilot of a research probe entering the Jupiter system. Your mission: survey Jupiter and its four largest moons — Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto — collecting scientific data along the way.

At each destination, scan the surface, atmosphere, and science highlights, record your findings in the Mission Log, then complete a knowledge challenge to unlock the next moon. Along the way you'll uncover why this giant planet and its moons matter for understanding how our Solar System formed — and where else in it life might exist.
